# Morphium Risk Register (Physical Phase)

| ID | Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **R1** | **IGZO Stoichiometry Drift** | Critical | High | Use PLD with a single dense target (InGaZnO4) instead of co-sputtering. |
| **R2** | **H2Se Toxicity** | Fatal | Low | Do NOT use H2Se gas. Use Thermal Evaporation of elemental Se in a vacuum chamber. |
| **R3** | **Dielectric Breakdown** | High | Medium | Use ALD for HfO2 to ensure pinhole-free films. If sputtering, use thick (100nm) layers for v0. |
| **R4** | **Stiction in Latch** | Medium | High | Apply hydrophobic coating (FDTS) or use rough surfaces to minimize contact area. |
| **R5** | **Budget Overrun** | High | Medium | Stick to "Garage Fab" processes (Sputter/PLD). Avoid E-Beam Lithography ($$$). Use Maskless ($). |
| **R6** | **Sc Target Cost** | Medium | Low | Buy AlSc alloy target (cheaper) instead of pure Sc target. |
