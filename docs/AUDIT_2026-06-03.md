# Morphium Kernel — Truthfulness Audit (2026-06-03)

Adversarial audit of every simulator's physics and every calibration number
against peer-reviewed literature: 7 independent auditors (5 layer-physics + 2
methodology/numbers), each finding re-verified by a separate skeptic.
**39 findings checked → 26 confirmed, 13 rejected as unsubstantiated.**

## Bottom line
The core materials physics (L/PM/E/EM) and the optimization methodology are
sound and literature-grounded. The problems were concentrated in the M-foglet
layer (a heuristic stub), one critical mis-attribution bug, the EM calibration
(biased low), and stale docs. **All 26 confirmed findings are resolved** (see
resolution table). 13 allegations — including 6 of 8 methodology claims — were
rejected by independent verification (e.g. "process noise double-counted",
"multiplicative model-risk Gaussian wrong", "additive phi can't fix PM bias"
were all shown false), so severity was not inflated.
